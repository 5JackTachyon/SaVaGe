The WAR Of The GWAN

In the year 2025, Hostgator has become more powerful than ever. It has absorbed Github, Backendless, SourceTree, Unity, Netbeans, Atom, App Wiki, the App Academy Site, PHS website, and millions more. Sean Dow Miller tried to warn them, but they didn't listen. Devin Inc was at an all time high, the clone business was booming, machines were too unpredictable and have already tried to take over the world in the year 2020 but Devin Inc's military force came in and saved the day. Devin, the direct of Devin Incorporated, never expected a 70 foot tall beast of reptilainne being to attack, but he was ready, he sold military clones to the military so he had a supply of top notch military weaponry.
